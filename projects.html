NCAA Outdoor Track & Field Database

A PostgreSQL project driven by my passion for track and field and analytics. This comprehensive database encompasses results from 2015 to 2023, aiming to go beyond individual and team champions and uncover hidden patterns and trends in times, overall team points, and progression in the sport. With a focus on providing valuable insights to recruits and shedding light on lesser-known data, this project showcases expertise in database development, schema design, and query optimization to produce results. Harnessing the power of applications like VS Code, GitHub, PgAdmin, and DBeaver, this database project offers a gateway to empower the track and field community with valuable insights and fostering a deeper understanding of the highest caliber meets.
